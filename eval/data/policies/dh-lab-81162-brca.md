# POLICY: DH-LAB-81162-BRCA
payer: DemoHealth
service: 81162
indication: Z15.01
title: BRCA1/BRCA2 Full-Sequence Analysis — Hereditary Risk Criteria

## C1: Hereditary risk criteria
The member must have a personal or family history of breast, ovarian, or
pancreatic cancer meeting hereditary risk assessment criteria.

## C2: Genetic counseling
The member must have completed pre-test genetic counseling with a qualified
genetics professional.

## C3: First-time testing
The laboratory record must be reviewed to confirm this is the first full
sequence analysis of the BRCA1 and BRCA2 genes for this member.

## C4: Ordering provider
The test must be ordered by a physician or genetic counselor managing the
member's hereditary cancer risk assessment.
