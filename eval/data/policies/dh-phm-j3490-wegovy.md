# POLICY: DH-PHM-J3490-WEGOVY
payer: DemoHealth
service: J3490
indication: E66.9
title: Wegovy (semaglutide) — Chronic Weight Management Criteria

## C1: BMI threshold
The member must have a documented body mass index of at least 30, or a body
mass index of at least 27 accompanied by a weight-related comorbid condition
such as hypertension or type 2 diabetes.

## C2: Structured weight-management program
The member must have participated in a structured weight management or
lifestyle modification program lasting at least 12 weeks before starting
pharmacotherapy.

## C3: Thyroid history screening
Personal and family history must be screened for medullary thyroid carcinoma
and multiple endocrine neoplasia type 2 before treatment begins.

## C4: Baseline measurement
A baseline weight and body mass index measurement must be recorded in the
chart during the 3 months before the request.
