# POLICY: DH-PHM-J0135-PSA
payer: DemoHealth
service: J0135
indication: L40.50
title: Humira (adalimumab) — Psoriatic Arthritis Criteria

## C1: Diagnosis
The member must have active psoriatic arthritis diagnosed by a rheumatology
or dermatology specialist.

## C2: Prior therapy trial
The member must have completed a trial of nonsteroidal therapy or a
conventional disease modifying agent lasting at least 8 weeks.

## C3: Tuberculosis screening
Tuberculosis screening must be completed during the 12 months before biologic
initiation.
