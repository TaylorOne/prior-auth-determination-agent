# POLICY: DH-PHM-1599538-AFIB
payer: DemoHealth
service: 1599538
indication: I48.91
title: Xarelto (rivaroxaban) — Non-Valvular Atrial Fibrillation Criteria

## C1: Confirmed diagnosis
The member must have a documented diagnosis of non-valvular atrial
fibrillation confirmed by electrocardiogram or cardiology consultation.

## C2: Stroke risk score
The member must have a documented stroke risk score of at least 2 using the
standard atrial fibrillation stroke risk scale.

## C3: Renal function
Renal function must be assessed with a creatinine clearance or eGFR result
recorded during the 12 months before initiation.

## C4: Anticoagulant review
Current medications must be reviewed to confirm the member is free of
concurrent therapeutic anticoagulant agents.
