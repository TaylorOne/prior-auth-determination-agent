# POLICY: DH-RAD-73721-KNEE
payer: DemoHealth
service: 73721
indication: M25.561
title: MRI Knee Without Contrast — Medical Necessity Criteria

## C1: Conservative therapy completed
The member must have completed a course of conservative therapy for the knee,
such as supervised physical therapy, structured exercise, NSAIDs, or a
corticosteroid injection.

## C2: Therapy duration
The completed conservative therapy course must have lasted a minimum of 6 weeks.

## C3: Persistent symptoms
Knee pain symptoms must persist or worsen despite the completed conservative
therapy course.

## C4: Prior radiograph
A plain radiograph (X-ray) of the knee must have been performed and reviewed
prior to advanced imaging.
