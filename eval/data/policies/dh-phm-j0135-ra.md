# POLICY: DH-PHM-J0135-RA
payer: DemoHealth
service: J0135
indication: M06.9
title: Humira (adalimumab) — Rheumatoid Arthritis Criteria

## C1: Diagnosis and severity
The member must have moderately to severely active rheumatoid arthritis
diagnosed by a rheumatology specialist.

## C2: Conventional DMARD trial
The member must have completed a trial of a conventional disease modifying
antirheumatic drug such as methotrexate lasting at least 12 weeks.

## C3: Inadequate response
The chart must document an inadequate response or intolerance to the
conventional disease modifying therapy.

## C4: Tuberculosis screening
Tuberculosis screening with an interferon gamma release assay or tuberculin
skin test must be completed during the 12 months before biologic initiation.
